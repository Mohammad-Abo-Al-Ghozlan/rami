About Us

Afkar Altatweer Contracting Co. (ATCE) is committed to providing construction services with the highest quality of international standards. The management takes full responsibility to ensure the complete satisfaction of our customers.

We take great pride in the work we have accomplished in the themed and Specialty Sector. It's a unique opportunity for us to display the full extent of our expertise in bespoke, creative and technically challenging projects.

Our commitment towards "Building Excellence" goes beyond premium quality projects. We build excellence when going the extra mile to help achieve goals, by sharing your commitment and facing your barriers as our own.

Our Services

Site Management

Initiation, Planning, Execution, Monitoring and Performance, Quality Assurance and Closure. Our team ensures your project runs smoothly with focus on productivity, efficiency, quality, health and safety.

Fit Out & MEP

We carry later stages planning side by side with construction planning from the first day, to keep things neat and in sync, preventing delays at fit out and MEP stages.

Project Management

From minor tasks to a broad overview of your project, our team helps you achieve the best possible solution for flexible and fail-proof schedule, solving problems instead of just pointing them out.

Integrated Design

Through transparent communication and a holistic approach to the building's life cycle, we arrive at unique design solutions that are cost effective in both the short and long run.

Live Mock-ups

Our fabrication facilities provide you with a tangible vision of what the final product will look like, through the creation of real size mock-ups, reducing risks in material selection.

Ethical Leadership

Working with different companies has brought our ethical standards to an International level. We respect your values and hold ourselves accountable for matching them.
